Create a proposal or vote one

Make or vote a proposal: A proposal is equivalent to a project pitch and includes details about the proposal such as objectives, methods, costs and milestones. You can vote on whether a proposal should be accepted or rejected.
Users can increase their reputation by: Creating proposals that get accepted by the DAO or voting in favor of proposals that get accepted by the DAO.
Users’ reputation might decrease by: Creating a proposal that does not get accepted by the DAO or voting for a proposal that does not get accepted by the DAO.
